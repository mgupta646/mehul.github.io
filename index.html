About Me

Hola! 👋 I'm Mehul Gupta, a graduate student at NYU Tandon School of Engineering pursuing my Master's in Management of Technology. My journey began with Aerospace Engineering, where I built a solid technical foundation in systems design, simulations, and rocket propulsion.

Over time, I expanded my focus to the intersection of technology, business, and management, aiming to lead transformative projects in data-driven industries.

I bring a unique blend of engineering expertise and management skills — from leading large-scale technical teams (like the Guinness World Record Hydro Rocket Project) to driving international collaborations as President of Chandigarh University's Astronomy Club.

Outside academics, you'll find me exploring sci-fi novels, documentaries, and global innovation trends.
